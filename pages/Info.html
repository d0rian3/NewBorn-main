ABOUT LARINA ELENA



Hi! My name is Larina Elena and I am a child, family and newborn photographer with over 3 years of experience. I have been lucky enough to work with many families and capture the most precious moments in their lives - from the first days of a newborn baby to warm family gatherings. I am originally from Kazakhstan, but now I live and work in beautiful Prague. As a mom of three children, I understand how important sincere and emotional moments are, so I try to convey them in each of my photos. For me a photo shoot is not just a job, but an opportunity to preserve soulful memories that will delight you for years to come. My main goal is to create a warm and cozy atmosphere for you at the shoot, so that everyone feels comfortable and natural. I love to work with children, to find an approach to everyone, and even the most shy kids open up in the frame. Welcome to the world of child and family photography, where I will help you preserve your family's unique moments!
